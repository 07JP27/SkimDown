# Emoji Shortcodes

SkimDown supports GitHub-style emoji shortcodes. Type a colon `:`, the emoji name, and another colon `:`.

## Faces and Emotions

:smile: :laughing: :blush: :heart_eyes: :sunglasses:

:cry: :sob: :angry: :scream: :thinking:

:wink: :stuck_out_tongue: :zany_face: :shushing_face: :nerd_face:
