## Using Headings

h1 and h2 display an underline (`border-bottom`). It's common to use h1 for the document title, h2 for major sections, and h3 for subsections.

### Subsection Example

This is a paragraph under an h3 heading.

#### Deeper Section

h4 and below are used for smaller section divisions.

##### Supplementary Heading

h5 is for supplementary information.

###### Smallest Heading

h6 is the smallest heading level.
