# Horizontal Rules

Horizontal rules are used to separate sections. They can be created with three or more hyphens, asterisks, or underscores.

## Hyphens `---`

Section above

---

Section below

## Asterisks `***`

Section above

***

Section below

## Underscores `___`

Section above

___

Section below
